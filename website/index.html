Features

Auto Capture

Every closed tab is automatically recorded — title, URL, favicon, and domain. Nothing is lost.

Smart Grouping

View by time (Just now / Today / Yesterday) or by domain. Batch-closed tabs are auto-grouped.

Pin & Keep

Pin important tabs to keep them safe from auto-cleanup. They stay until you say otherwise.

Instant Search

Search by title, URL, or domain. Results update as you type.

One-Click Restore

Restore single tabs or entire batch-closed groups with one click.

Theme Switching

Light, Dark, or follow your system. Switch anytime.

Screenshots

Stop losing your tabs.
